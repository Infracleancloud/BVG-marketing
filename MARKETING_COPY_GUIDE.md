# Marketing Copy Guide (Palantir-Style)

This guide defines the voice, structure, and standards for the Infra Clean Cloud marketing site.

## Voice and Tone
- Executive, precise, and outcomes-first
- Confident, measured, and evidence-led
- Avoid hype, avoid slang, avoid marketing fluff

## Narrative Principles
- Lead with mission and outcomes, not features
- Emphasize governance, control, auditability, and accountability
- Use measurable results and operational language
- Show "system of record" reliability, not surface UX

## Section Integrity (Required Per Page)
Every page must include, at minimum:
1. Executive framing (why it matters)
2. Operating model (how it works in practice)
3. Proof or metrics (quantified impact)
4. Clear CTA (briefing, assessment, or contact)

## Preferred Section Types
- Governance narrative
- Operational cadence (timeline/steps)
- Executive metrics (stat blocks)
- Trust signals (security, compliance, reliability)
- Role alignment (priorities + outcomes)
- Proof (case studies, benchmarks)

## Language Guidelines
Use: governance, control, posture, accountability, audit-ready, evidence, operating model, remediation cadence  
Avoid: “easy”, “simple”, “fun”, “magic”, “growth hack”

## CTA Guidelines
- Primary CTA: “Request briefing”
- Secondary CTA: “Explore platform” or “View outcomes”
- Keep CTA text short and executive-focused

## Do / Don’t
Do:
- Use short, declarative sentences
- Provide measurable outcomes
- Anchor claims in operations

Don’t:
- Overuse adjectives
- Make unqualified claims
- Use casual or playful phrasing
